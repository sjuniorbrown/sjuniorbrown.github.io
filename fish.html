Fish For Fitness


I was on the team that developed this site. This was yet another e-commerce site built using opencart. This site was much easier to build as I had began to understand the complex structure that opencart uses and what modules go where. I was tasked with developing the sites product page and giving functionality to the sites blog, so that the client could update their customers on what was going on inside the firm. I also helped one of the developers on my team with the development of the checkout.

 

This site took 1 month and 2 weeks to develop. I used HTML, CSS, Javasscript, PHP and AJAX.
